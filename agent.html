About TruCash

📌 What is TruCash?

TruCash is a Zambian‑registered microfinance institution dedicated to providing fast, collateral‑based loans to individuals and small businesses. We leverage technology to make credit accessible while ensuring responsible lending.

🤝 Benefits of Being an Agent

  • Earn 15% commission on every interest payment from customers you refer.
  • Recurring income – as long as your customers repay, you earn.
  • No capital required – you simply connect borrowers with TruCash.
  • Full support – marketing materials, training, and a dedicated agent dashboard.
  • Transparent tracking – see exactly how much you’ve earned and what’s pending.
