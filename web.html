How It Works

Collateral Loans Explained

At TruCash, we offer quick loans secured by your valuable assets. No credit history required — just bring your collateral and get cash within hours.

What Can You Use as Collateral?

Vehicles (cars, motorcycles, trucks), electronics (laptops, smartphones, tablets), home appliances (TVs, fridges), and other valuable assets with clear ownership.

Simple & Transparent Interest

We charge simple interest only: 10% for 1‑week loans and 35% for 1‑month loans. No compounding, no processing fees, no hidden charges.

Fast Approval & Disbursement

Apply in the TruCash app, submit collateral photos and details. Our agents verify within hours, and funds are sent directly to your mobile money or bank account.

Company

Why TruCash?

TruCash is a Zambian-registered financial technology company focused on providing accessible, collateral-based loans to individuals and small businesses. We believe that everyone deserves fair credit, regardless of their banking history.

Unlike traditional lenders, we do not rely on credit scores. Instead, we accept physical assets as security — cars, electronics, appliances, and more. This allows us to approve loans quickly and disburse funds within hours.

Our interest rates are simple and transparent: 10% for 1-week loans and 35% for 1-month loans. There are no hidden fees, no prepayment penalties, and no compounding. What you see is what you pay.

We operate through a network of trusted agents across Zambia who assist borrowers with the application process, verify collateral, and ensure smooth communication. Agents earn 15% commission on every interest payment from customers they refer.

TruCash is fully licensed and compliant with Zambian microfinance regulations. Your assets are stored securely, and all transactions are encrypted.
